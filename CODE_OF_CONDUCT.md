# Code of Conduct - SEVE Framework

## Our Pledge

We, as members, contributors, and leaders of the SEVE Framework project, pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Examples of Behavior that Contributes to a Positive Environment

- **Being respectful** of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- **Giving and gracefully accepting** constructive feedback
- **Accepting responsibility** and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes
- **Focusing on what is best** for the community and the SEVE Framework
- **Showing empathy** towards other community members
- **Being ethical** in all interactions and contributions
- **Being transparent** about conflicts of interest
- **Being collaborative** and helping others succeed

### Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment** of any kind, including sexual harassment
- **Discrimination** based on any protected characteristic
- **Trolling, insulting, or derogatory** comments
- **Public or private harassment**
- **Publishing others' private information** without explicit permission
- **Conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate** in a professional setting
- **Unethical behavior** that violates SEVE Framework principles
- **Misuse of AI technology** for harmful purposes
- **Violation of privacy** or data protection principles

## Ethical AI Principles

As contributors to an ethical AI framework, we also commit to:

### Privacy and Data Protection
- **Respect user privacy** in all contributions
- **Protect personal data** according to best practices
- **Implement privacy-by-design** principles
- **Transparent data handling** practices

### Fairness and Non-Discrimination
- **Avoid bias** in algorithms and data
- **Ensure equal treatment** regardless of protected characteristics
- **Test for fairness** in all AI systems
- **Promote diversity** in development and usage

### Transparency and Accountability
- **Make decisions explainable** and auditable
- **Document ethical considerations** in all contributions
- **Take responsibility** for AI system outcomes
- **Enable human oversight** and control

### Safety and Beneficence
- **Prevent harm** to individuals and society
- **Design for safety** from the ground up
- **Consider unintended consequences** of AI systems
- **Promote beneficial uses** of AI technology

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. Examples of representing our community include using an official project e-mail address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.

## Reporting Violations

### How to Report

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it:

- **Email**: conduct@symbeon-tech.com
- **GitHub**: Create a private issue or contact maintainers directly
- **Discord**: Contact moderators privately
- **In Person**: Contact event organizers or community leaders

### What to Include

When reporting, please include:

- **Description** of the incident
- **Date and time** of the incident
- **Location** (online or offline)
- **People involved** (if known)
- **Witnesses** (if any)
- **Impact** on you or others
- **Desired outcome** (if any)

### Confidentiality

All reports will be handled confidentially. We will not share your identity without your explicit permission, except when required by law or to protect the safety of others.

## Enforcement Guidelines

### Community Impact Guidelines

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct:

#### 1. Correction
**Community Impact**: Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome in the community.

**Consequence**: A private, written warning from community leaders, providing clarity around the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

#### 2. Warning
**Community Impact**: A violation through a single incident or series of actions.

**Consequence**: A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, for a specified period of time. This includes avoiding interactions in community spaces as well as external channels like social media. Violating these terms may lead to a temporary or permanent ban.

#### 3. Temporary Ban
**Community Impact**: A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence**: A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community for a specified period of time. No public or private interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, is allowed during this period. Violating these terms may lead to a permanent ban.

#### 4. Permanent Ban
**Community Impact**: Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment of an individual, or aggression toward or disparagement of classes of individuals.

**Consequence**: A permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within the community.

### Ethical Violations

For violations related to ethical AI principles:

#### Minor Violations
- **Unintentional bias** in code or documentation
- **Inadequate privacy protection** in contributions
- **Missing ethical considerations** in new features

**Consequence**: Education and guidance on ethical AI principles, with requirement to address the issue.

#### Major Violations
- **Intentional bias** or discrimination
- **Privacy violations** or data misuse
- **Harmful AI applications** or misuse

**Consequence**: Temporary or permanent ban, depending on severity and intent.

## Appeals Process

If you disagree with a moderation decision:

1. **Contact the moderation team** within 7 days
2. **Provide additional context** or evidence
3. **Request a review** by a different moderator
4. **Escalate to project maintainers** if needed
